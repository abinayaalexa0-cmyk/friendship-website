Memories

I still remember that simple day when we went to the panipuri shop together with our friends. It wasn’t a big event, just a normal evening, but it became a memory I will never forget.
Those lunch breaks we spent together may have seemed small, but they meant a lot. Sitting together, sharing food, and laughing made everything feel lighter.
We used to talk about dramas for so long. It was never boring, because it wasn’t about the topic, it was about how comfortable we were.
You listened to me again and again, without judging or getting tired. That kind of support is something I will always be thankful for.
When I look back, I realize something… our friendship was built on simple moments, and that is what makes it special.

Apology

My dear best friend, I want to say this honestly from my heart… I am truly sorry. There were moments when I should have been there more, times when I should have spoken earlier, and things I should have made more special for you. I didn’t fully realize their importance at that time, but now I understand. Sometimes life becomes busy, and without noticing, we start missing the small moments that actually matter the most. For those missed moments, I am really sorry. Even when I was silent or not fully present, you were never far from my thoughts. You have always been someone who listens, understands, and never judges. You gave me your time and patience so easily, and I know I wasn’t always able to give the same back at the right time. But one thing I want you to know clearly… you have always mattered to me. Our friendship means a lot to me, even if I don’t always express it perfectly. I will try to be better, not just in words, but in actions. With a sincere heart, Your best friend 🖤❤️
