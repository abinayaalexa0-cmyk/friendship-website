A Letter Only For You

My dear best friend,

Before I say anything else, I want to say something from my heart. I am truly sorry that I couldn't celebrate your birthday the way you deserved. I know your birthday has already passed, and I wish I could have been there, wished you earlier, or done something special on that day itself.

Sometimes life becomes busy and complicated, and time moves faster than we expect. But even though the day passed, my thoughts about you never did.

That is the reason this small website exists. It may not be a big gift, but it is something I made carefully, thinking about you and our friendship.

Nine years is a long time. When we first became friends, we probably didn’t imagine that our friendship would last this long or become this meaningful.

There are many people we meet in life, but only a few become part of our story. You are one of those rare people in my life.

I still remember the small moments that made our friendship special. The simple days when we sat together, ate lunch, laughed, and talked about dramas.

I also remember the day we went to the panipuri shop with our friends. It was just a simple evening, eating panipuri and mushroom together and enjoying the moment.

You always listened. You always supported me. And you believed in my dreams even when others doubted them.

You are calm, kind, supportive, and someone who never judges others.

Even if we don’t talk every day or meet often, our friendship is still there.

You have big dreams too — making your parents proud and traveling the world.

I truly hope every one of those dreams becomes real for you.

Happy Birthday.

I hope this year brings you happiness and success.

Thank you for being someone who listens, believes, and supports.

And thank you for being part of my life.
