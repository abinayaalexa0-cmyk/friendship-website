Memories

I still remember that simple day when we went to the panipuri shop together with our friends. It wasn’t a big event, just a normal evening, but it became a memory I will never forget.
Those lunch breaks we spent together may have seemed small, but they meant a lot. Sitting together, sharing food, and laughing made everything feel lighter.
We used to talk about dramas for so long. It was never boring, because it wasn’t about the topic, it was about how comfortable we were.
You listened to me again and again, without judging or getting tired. That kind of support is something I will always be thankful for.
When I look back, I realize something… our friendship was built on simple moments, and that is what makes it special.

Little You

Your Dreams

One day, all your efforts will turn into success, and you will look back and feel proud of how far you came.

Our Future 🌍

My dear best friend, Sometimes I sit quietly and think about the future, and in almost every happy thought, you are there. I imagine a day where we finally get the chance to travel together, not just for a short time, but freely, without worries. We go to the places we once talked about, the places that felt like dreams when we said their names. I can already imagine us walking through beautiful streets, laughing about small things, taking pictures, and enjoying moments without even realizing how special they are. Just like before, but in a different place, in a different time. We once talked about visiting places like Tawang, Finland, and even Japan. Right now, they may feel far away, but I believe one day, we will stand there together and remember how we once only dreamed about it. More than the place, what matters to me is that you are there. Because even the most beautiful place would not feel the same without you. I want us to spend time together again, talk without limits, laugh without reasons, and create new memories that we will carry for the rest of our lives. Maybe life is busy now, and maybe things are not easy, but I still believe in that future. A future where we travel together. A future where we create more memories. A future where our friendship continues just like it always has. Until that day comes, I will keep that dream safely in my heart. Your best friend 🖤❤️

Apology

My dear best friend, I want to say this honestly from my heart… I am truly sorry. There were moments when I should have been there more, times when I should have spoken earlier, and things I should have made more special for you. I didn’t fully realize their importance at that time, but now I understand. Sometimes life becomes busy, and without noticing, we start missing the small moments that actually matter the most. For those missed moments, I am really sorry. Even when I was silent or not fully present, you were never far from my thoughts. You have always been someone who listens, understands, and never judges. You gave me your time and patience so easily, and I know I wasn’t always able to give the same back at the right time. But one thing I want you to know clearly… you have always mattered to me. Our friendship means a lot to me, even if I don’t always express it perfectly. I will try to be better, not just in words, but in actions. With a sincere heart, Your best friend 🖤❤️

Birthday

My dear best friend, First, I want to say I am truly sorry that I couldn’t celebrate your birthday the way you deserved. I couldn’t be there or wish you at the right time, and that makes me feel a little sad. Even though I wasn’t there, you were always in my thoughts. You are not just a friend to me. You are someone who has been part of my memories, my laughter, and so many simple moments that became special because of you. There are many people we meet in life, but only a few become truly important. You are one of those rare people for me. So even if this wish is a little late, I want to say it clearly… Happy Birthday. I hope this year brings you happiness, peace, and success. I hope your dreams slowly become real and your life is filled with beautiful moments. Thank you for being someone who listens, understands, and supports without judging. And please remember… You are appreciated. You are valued. And you mean a lot to me. Once again, Happy Birthday. With all my heart, Your best friend 🖤❤️
